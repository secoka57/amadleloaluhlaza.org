Our Vision

To provide educational development to all learners regardless of colour, gender and religion in accordance with the needs of the community as they are dictated by new demands of the new democratic South Africa informed by African renaissance.

Our Mission

Amadlelo Aluhlaza Maths, Science and Technology Academy is working together with all stakeholders and is committed to striving to be a centre of excellent quality education by providing support based on the principles of Batho Pele through capacity building, empowerment and maximum utilization of existing resources.
