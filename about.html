About Amadlelo Aluhlaza Secondary School

Amadlelo Aluhlaza Secondary School is dedicated to providing high-quality education to all learners regardless of colour, gender, or religion. We work closely with our community and stakeholders to nurture academic growth, personal development, and social responsibility in line with the evolving needs of South Africa and the spirit of the African Renaissance.

Mayelana ne-Amadlelo Aluhlaza Secondary School

Isikole i-Amadlelo Aluhlaza Secondary School sizibophezele ekunikezeni imfundo esezingeni eliphezulu kubo bonke abafundi ngaphandle kokubandlulula ngokobuhlanga, ubulili, noma inkolo. Sisebenzisana ngokuseduze nomphakathi kanye nabo bonke ababambiqhaza ukukhulisa ukukhula kwezemfundo, ukuthuthukiswa komuntu siqu, kanye nomthwalo wemfanelo emphakathini ngokuhambisana nezidingo ezishintshashintshayo zeNingizimu Afrika kanye nomoya we-African Renaissance.

Our Vision

To provide educational development to all learners regardless of colour, gender and religion in accordance with the needs of the community as they are dictated by new demands of the new democratic South Africa informed by African renaissance.

Umbono Wethu

Ukuhlinzeka ngentuthuko yemfundo kubo bonke abafundi ngaphandle kokubandlulula ngokobuhlanga, ubulili noma inkolo ngokuhambisana nezidingo zomphakathi njengoba ziqondiswa izidingo ezintsha zeNingizimu Afrika entsha enombuso wobumbano oqondiswa i-African Renaissance.

Our Mission

Amadlelo Aluhlaza Maths, Science and Technology Academy is working together with all stakeholders and is committed to striving to be a centre of excellent quality education by providing support based on the principles of Batho Pele through capacity building, empowerment and maximum utilization of existing resources.

Umsebenzi Wethu

I-Amadlelo Aluhlaza Maths, Science and Technology Academy isebenzisana nabo bonke ababambiqhaza futhi izibophezele ekuzameni ukuba yisikhungo semfundo esezingeni eliphezulu ngokuhlinzeka ngosizo olusekelwe ezimisweni zeBatho Pele ngokwakha amakhono, ukunikwa amandla kanye nokusetshenziswa okukhulu kwezinsiza ezikhona.
