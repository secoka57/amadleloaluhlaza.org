Amadlelo Aluhlaza Secondary School

2025 Winter Camp Boarding Rules

Code of Conduct & Discipline

  • House mothers and fathers are responsible for hostel discipline.
  • Initiation in any form is strictly prohibited.
  • No fighting or physical handling among boarders.
  • Boarders may not smoke, consume any alcoholic beverages, or possess any tobacco or drugs.
  • No sexual or indecent physical contact between learners is allowed.
  • No climbing through hostel windows, roofs, or using unauthorized entrances.
  • Treat all hostel staff, educators, and learners with respect and dignity.

Clothing & Laundry

All clothing should be clearly marked. Only age-appropriate and decent clothes are allowed. Avoid bringing expensive clothes.

Dirty laundry can be submitted daily between 08:00 and 16:00. Hanging wet clothes in the windows is not allowed.

Class Attendance & Study Times

  • Class attendance is compulsory.
  • Learners may not be in the hostel during class times.
  • No talking, eating, walking, or moving around during study time without permission.
  • Learners must sit in their assigned seats and leave study areas clean.

Meal Times

  • Breakfast: 06:00 – 07:00
  • Lunch: 12:00 – 13:00
  • Dinner: 17:00 – 18:00

The schedule may change based on the timetable. No hats or talking allowed in the dining hall. Learners must not take utensils to the dorms.

Hostel Routines

  • Lights out: 22:00
  • Wake-up time: 04:30
  • Learners must keep their rooms clean.
  • Entry into other learners’ rooms is not allowed without permission.
  • Learners should bring their own toiletries, towels, sanitary items, and toilet paper.

Prohibited Behaviors

  • Stealing, fighting, violence, or sexual misconduct
  • Vandalism, bullying, racism, hate speech, or harassment
  • Absconding from the hostel or entering wrong dormitories
  • Gang formation, pornography distribution, or alcohol possession
  • Smoking, sleeping outside without permission, or moving around at night without authorization

Signed by: Ms. P.E.T Mahlangu (Principal)
