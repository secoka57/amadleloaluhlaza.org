Learner Code of Conduct

At Amadlelo Aluhlaza Secondary School, we expect all learners to adhere to the highest standards of behavior to create a positive and safe learning environment.

General Expectations

  • Respect for teachers, staff, peers, and property.
  • Attend all classes punctually and regularly.
  • Wear the school uniform properly and with pride.
  • Complete all assignments and homework on time.
  • Maintain honesty and integrity in all school activities.

Esikoleni sase-Amadlelo Aluhlaza Secondary School, silindele ukuthi bonke abafundi balandele izinga eliphezulu lokuziphatha ukuze kwakhiwe indawo yokufunda enhle nephaphelekileyo.

Izinto Ezilindelekile Ngokujwayelekile

  • Hlonipha othisha, abasebenzi, ozakwabo, kanye nezimpahla.
  • Hamba zonke izifundo ngesikhathi nangokujwayelekile.
  • Gqoka isudi yesikole kahle nangokuzigqaja.
  • Qeda yonke imisebenzi nesabelo ngesikhathi.
  • Gcina ubuqotho nokwethembeka kuzo zonke izenzo zesikole.

Prohibited Behaviors

  • Bullying, harassment, or intimidation of others.
  • Use or possession of illegal substances or weapons.
  • Damage or theft of school or personal property.
  • Disruptive behavior in class or school premises.
  • Use of offensive language or gestures.

Consequences

Violations of the code of conduct will result in disciplinary actions, which may include warnings, detention, suspension, or expulsion depending on the severity.

Ukuziphatha Okungavunyelwe

  • Ukuhlukumeza, ukuthukuthela noma ukwesabisa abanye.
  • Ukusetshenziswa noma ukuba nezidakamizwa ezingekho emthethweni noma izikhali.
  • Ukwonakala noma ukweba izimpahla zesikole noma zomuntu siqu.
  • Ukuziphatha okuphazamisayo ekilasini noma esikoleni.
  • Ukusebenzisa ulimi noma izenzo ezingcolile.

Izinxephezelo

Ukwephula ikhodi yokuziphatha kuzoholela ezenzweni zokuziphatha, okungenzeka kube izixwayiso, ukuvalelwa, ukuvinjelwa, noma ukuhlukaniswa kuye ngokuthi kuyingozi kangakanani.
